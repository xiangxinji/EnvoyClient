# AI Assistant

AI assistant capabilities span multiple feature modules, providing intelligent assistance.

## AI Auto Reply

When enabled, AI automatically generates and sends a reply after receiving a message from another member, with a 5-second debounce per conversation. Channel messages do not trigger auto reply. Can be toggled from the settings panel or via keyboard shortcut.

## AI Chat Suggestions

Click the lightning button in the chat panel to have AI stream a reply suggestion based on recent chat context. You can accept the suggestion to fill the input box, or regenerate for a new suggestion.

## AI Task Analysis

Leaders can use AI to analyze task execution results and receive intelligent task assessments and recommendations.

## Leader AI Review

From the Leader's perspective, AI automatically reviews members' execution results after task completion, returning a review conclusion (pass/fail) and summary. The Leader can also manually approve or reject.
