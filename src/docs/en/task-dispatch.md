# Task Dispatch

Task dispatch is a Leader-only feature that uses AI to intelligently assign tasks to suitable team members.

## Online Members

The top of the panel shows currently online team members. Each member card displays their responsibilities and capability tags, helping the Leader understand team availability.

## AI Smart Dispatch

Enter a task description in the text box and click "AI Smart Dispatch". AI analyzes online members' responsibilities and capabilities, automatically matching the most suitable candidates and generating a task plan.

## Serial and Parallel

After AI returns the dispatch preview, you can choose the execution mode: serial mode (execute sequentially one by one) or parallel mode (execute simultaneously). Choose the appropriate mode based on task dependencies.

## Confirm and Cancel

The preview card shows AI-generated task content, assigned members, and their responsibilities. Confirm to send the task to selected members; cancel to discard the dispatch.
